Every kid deserves a better future

At the Amogelang Bana Foundation, we believe every child deserves a future full of hope, love, and opportunity.

Our Mission

We are dedicated to nurturing the lives of children in underserved communities by providing essential support services such as education, nutrition, emotional care, and skills development.

What We Do

Feeding Program

We serve over 300 meals daily to children in need, ensuring they grow strong and healthy.

Early Childhood Education

We run early learning classes that equip young children with foundational skills before primary school.

After-school Tutoring

Our volunteer tutors help learners with homework, exam prep, and literacy.
