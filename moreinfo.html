Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q: Is the foundation a registered nonprofit?
    A: Yes, we are a registered NPO in South Africa
  • Q: Can I volunteer?
    A: Absolutely! We need volunteers for tutoring, food distribution, administration, and events.
  • Q: Do you accept donations?
    A: Yes. We accept financial donations, food items, clothes, books, and school supplies.
  • Q: Where are you based?
    A: Our main center is in Johannesburg, South Africa. Visit our Contact Page for directions.
