About Us

Who We Are

The Amogelang Bana Foundation is a non-profit organization based in South Africa that is dedicated to improving the lives of vulnerable and orphaned children. We provide safe spaces, education, nutrition, and emotional support ‚Äî helping children not only survive, but thrive.

Our Mission

To nurture, protect, and empower children in need through community-driven programs focused on education, health, nutrition, and psychosocial support.

Our Vision

A world where every child has the opportunity to grow up in a loving environment, free from poverty, abuse, and neglect ‚Äî and equipped to reach their full potential.

Meet the Founder

Amogelang Poppy Mokau is the visionary behind the Amogelang Bana Foundation. Raised in a community affected by poverty and inequality, Amogelang understood firsthand the challenges vulnerable children face. Driven by compassion, she founded the organization in 2025 with the belief that no child should be left behind.

Her background in education, combined with a deep connection to local communities, has helped her grow the foundation from a small feeding program to a beacon of hope for hundreds of children. Her leadership continues to inspire volunteers, donors, and families every day.
